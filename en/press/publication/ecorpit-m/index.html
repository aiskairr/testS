Calving season - Ecorpit-M

29 april 2019

It’s time to start preparing for calving season. Being prepared can reduce the stress on the cow and calf and improve health.
Introducing Ecorpit-M, our feed additive that contains optimal balance of active ingredients required for cows in the first days after calving and horses after foaling. 

Composition and concentration of biologically active substances provide essential vitamins, macro and micro nutrients, and carbohydrates.

  • Prevention of postpartum stress
  • Prevention of parturient paresis
  • Activation of ruminal microbiota
  • Accelerated involution of reproductive organs
  • Increase in productivity
