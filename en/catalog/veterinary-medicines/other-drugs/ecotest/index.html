Ecotest

On-farm test for detection of mastitis

Surface-active substances, contained in Ecotest, interact with the nuclear DNA of milk somatic cells, which count level is increased when the inflammation process in the udder is present. As a result of the reaction clots of different densities and sizes are formed depending on the number of somatic cells. Test sensitivity is 170 000 (± 30 000) cells/ml of milk. The color of the mixture changes from pink to yellow in presence of an indicator.

Active ingredients

Each ml contains 30 mg of anionic surface-active substances, indicator, excipients, solvent.

Application

Ecotest is used for mastitis detection or evaluation of efficacy of the mastitis treatment based on the somatic cell count. It is useful screening tool to identify the inflammatory processes associated with subclinical, latent and chronic mastitis using milk samples. Ecotest is conducted from the 4th day after calving, during lactation, and before the dry period.
Clean the teat thoroughly with a teat wipe and milk out first streams of milk, as they contain a lot of bacteria. Use a plastic test paddle for milk samples collecting. The paddle has four shallow cups to help identify the individual quarter from which the milk was obtained. Milk samples (about 2 ml) from each quarter are collected in the clean paddle (up to graduation marks). Add an equal amount (about 2 ml) of Ecotest solution to each cup in the paddle. Rotate the paddle in a circular motion or use a glass rod to mix the contents. Do not mix more than 10-15 seconds.
The reaction is scored visually in 15-20 seconds: gel formation and change of color indicate increased count of somatic cells and probability of inflammation. See the table (Table No.1 - Scoring guide) to interpret a result.

MARKETING PACKAGING

Ecotest is marketed in glass or polymer bottles of 500 ml.

STORAGE CONDITIONS AND SHELF LIFE

Store in the original package between 5°C to 25°C, protected from light and moisture.
Shelf life of the veterinary product as packaged for sale: 1 year.
Do not use this veterinary product after the expiry date which is stated on the label.
